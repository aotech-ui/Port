Overview

AO-TECH provides custom software development, web application development, UI/UX design, abd related digital services. Due to the nature of these services - often tailored to specific client requirements-refunds are subject to specific conditions as outlined below.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be considered under the following circumstances:

  • The project has not yet commenced, and a refund request is submitted before work begins.
  • AO-TECH is unable to deliver the agreed services due to internal constraints.
  • A confirmed billing or payment error occured on our part.

All refund request are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

2. Non-Refundable Services

The following are non-refundable:

  • Completed or partiallyt completed software development work

Once development has started or deliverables have been shared, refunds will not bve issued.

3. Project Cancellation

If a client chooses to cancel a project after work has commenced:

4. Milstone-Based Projects

For projects structured around milstones:

  • Refunds may only be considered for unstarted milestones, subject to review and approval.
